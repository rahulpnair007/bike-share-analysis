🚲 Divvy Bike-Share Analysis
This project analyses bike-share trip data over the past 12 months to uncover usage trends.

Key Findings:
•	🚴‍♂️ Ride Lengths: Most rides are under 40 minutes, with few long rides.
•	🚲 Bike Types: Classic bikes were the most popular, followed by electric bikes.
•	📅 Days of Week: Highest usage observed on weekends (Saturday and Sunday).
•	🕒 Time of Day: Peak hours are early morning and late afternoon (commuting times).

Tools Used:
•	R
•	tidyverse
•	ggplot2
•	lubridate

Visualisations:
•	Ride Length Distribution
•	Rides by Bike Type
•	Rides by Weekday
